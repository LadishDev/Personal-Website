**FineTrack** is a cross-platform mobile app designed to help vehicle owners track fines, charges, MOT, insurance, and other vehicle-related expenses in one place. The app provides a secure, user-friendly dashboard for managing multiple vehicles, setting reminders, and analyzing spending trends.

## ğŸš— Why FineTrack?

Managing a vehicle involves juggling MOT renewals, insurance, fines, and various expenses. FineTrack was created to centralize all this information, making it easy to stay organized and avoid missed deadlines or unexpected costs.

## âœ¨ Features

- **Fine Tracking:** Log and categorize fines and charges with details like date, amount, and notes.
- **MOT & Insurance Reminders:** Set and receive notifications for upcoming renewals.
- **Expense Management:** Track repairs, fuel, maintenance, and other costs.
- **Dashboard Overview:** View a summary of vehicle status and upcoming deadlines.
- **Multi-Vehicle Support:** Manage several vehicles from a single account.
- **Data Privacy:** All data is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

### Supported Fine Types

- Speeding
- Parking
- Congestion/ULEZ/LEZ
- Bus Lane
- Red Light
- Toll/Bridge/Other
